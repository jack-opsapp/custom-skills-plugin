## Pages to Scan Per Competitor

When running a competitor scan, fetch these pages:
1. Homepage (main value prop, hero copy)
2. Pricing page (transparency, tiers, CTAs)
3. Features page (feature framing, language)
4. About page (mission, social proof numbers)
5. Any industry-specific landing pages (HVAC, plumbing, etc.)

## What to Analyze

### Copy Quality
- Hero headline: specific or generic?
- Value proposition: differentiated or "all-in-one"?
- Pain points named: real problems or feature lists?
- Tone: corporate SaaS or trade-authentic?
- CTA strength: "Book a demo" vs actionable

### Audience Targeting
- Who is the copy talking TO? (owner, admin, crew, foreman?)
- Trade-specific language or generic "field service"?
- Non-English support mentioned?
- Mobile-first positioning or desktop-first?

### Pricing Strategy
- Published or hidden?
- Per-user or flat rate?
- Add-on/upsell structure?
- Free trial or forced demo?

### Social Proof
- Customer count claims (verifiable?)
- Testimonials (from which roles?)
- Case studies with real numbers?
- Review site ratings?
