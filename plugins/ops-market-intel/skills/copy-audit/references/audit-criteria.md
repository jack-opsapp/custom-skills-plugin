# Copy Audit Scoring Criteria

## Anti-Pattern Checklist (Deduct Points)

Each anti-pattern detected reduces the score. Rate severity: HIGH (-5), MEDIUM (-3), LOW (-1).

### 1. Generic Language Traps
| Anti-Pattern | Severity | Why It's Bad |
|---|---|---|
| "All-in-one" | HIGH | 6/8 competitors use this exact phrase — zero differentiation |
| "Streamline operations" | HIGH | Every FSM competitor says this — invisible to readers |
| "Empower your team" | MEDIUM | Corporate SaaS speak, not trade language |
| "Delight your customers" | HIGH | Borrowed from consumer SaaS, alien to trade workers |
| "Grow your business" | MEDIUM | Generic, could be any industry |
| "Save time and money" | MEDIUM | Cliché, no specificity |
| "Boost efficiency" | MEDIUM | Abstract — what does this mean for a plumber at 6am? |

### 2. Audience Misalignment
| Anti-Pattern | Severity | Why It's Bad |
|---|---|---|
| Copy addresses only owners/admins | HIGH | Ignores 80% of daily users (crews, foremen) |
| No mention of field workers | HIGH | OPS's core differentiator is crew-first |
| "Your employees" framing | MEDIUM | Positions workers as objects, not users |
| No trade-specific scenarios | MEDIUM | Generic descriptions instead of real situations |

### 3. Feature Over Pain
| Anti-Pattern | Severity | Why It's Bad |
|---|---|---|
| Feature list without context | MEDIUM | "GPS tracking" means nothing without "know where your crew is" |
| Technical jargon | MEDIUM | "AI-powered dispatch optimization" vs "the right person at the right job" |
| Module/dashboard emphasis | LOW | Complexity signals — trade workers want simple |

### 4. Tone Problems
| Anti-Pattern | Severity | Why It's Bad |
|---|---|---|
| Corporate/enterprise voice | HIGH | Alienates blue-collar audience |
| Marketing buzzwords | MEDIUM | "Leverage", "synergy", "ecosystem" |
| Passive voice | LOW | "Operations are streamlined" vs "Get your crew moving" |
| Exclamation marks | LOW | Feels salesy, not professional |

### 5. Structural Issues
| Anti-Pattern | Severity | Why It's Bad |
|---|---|---|
| Hidden pricing / "Book a demo" | HIGH | 6/8 competitors do this — massive friction |
| Long paragraphs | MEDIUM | Trade workers scan, don't read blocks |
| No clear CTA | MEDIUM | What should the reader DO? |
| Hero section tries to say everything | MEDIUM | One message, one pain point, one CTA |

## Strength Checklist (Add Points)

Each strength present adds to the score. Rate impact: HIGH (+5), MEDIUM (+3), LOW (+1).

### 1. Crew-First Messaging (+5 each)
- [ ] Copy addresses field workers directly
- [ ] Mentions foremen/crew leaders as users
- [ ] Describes the field worker's daily experience
- [ ] Uses "your crew" not "your employees"

### 2. Real Pain Points Named (+5 each)
- [ ] Missed calls / lost revenue
- [ ] Slow quoting / on-site estimates
- [ ] Text-chain coordination chaos
- [ ] Spreadsheet/whiteboard scheduling
- [ ] Software too complex for crew adoption
- [ ] Software too expensive for small teams

### 3. Trade-Authentic Voice (+3 each)
- [ ] Short, direct sentences
- [ ] Active voice throughout
- [ ] Scenarios a trade worker recognizes ("6am dispatch", "gloves on")
- [ ] Professional but not corporate
- [ ] No marketing buzzwords

### 4. OPS Differentiators Present (+5 each)
- [ ] Transparent pricing visible
- [ ] "Download and use today" / instant access
- [ ] Mobile-native positioning (the app IS the product)
- [ ] Multi-trade inclusivity (not just HVAC/plumbing/electrical)
- [ ] Physical-reality awareness (gloves, sunlight, job sites)
- [ ] Simple by design (not ERP complexity)

### 5. SEO Alignment (+3 each)
- [ ] Target keywords naturally integrated
- [ ] Page targets a specific intent tier (buyer/problem/vertical)
- [ ] Trade-specific language that matches search queries
- [ ] Comparison/alternative positioning where appropriate

## Scoring Summary

**Maximum theoretical score:** 50 (all strengths, no anti-patterns)

| Score Range | Rating | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 40-50 | Excellent | Ship it |
| 30-39 | Good | Minor tweaks needed |
| 20-29 | Needs Work | Rewrite weak sections |
| 10-19 | Major Issues | Significant rewrite needed |
| 0-9 | Competitor-Grade | Full rewrite — sounds like everyone else |

## OPS Voice Examples

### Hero Headlines
**Strong:**
- "Built for the crew, not just the corner office."
- "Your crew wears gloves. Your software should know that."
- "One app. The whole job. From dispatch to done."
- "Stop paying for software your team won't use."

**Weak:**
- "The all-in-one field service management platform."
- "Streamline your operations with AI-powered tools."
- "Empowering field service professionals everywhere."

### Pain Point Copy
**Strong:**
- "60% of contractor calls go unanswered. Every one is money lost."
- "Your foreman is coordinating 8 guys via group text. There's a better way."
- "ServiceTitan costs $15,000/year and takes 6 months to set up. We cost less. We take 6 minutes."

**Weak:**
- "Reduce operational inefficiencies across your workforce."
- "Our platform helps you manage your field operations more effectively."
- "Improve customer satisfaction with better scheduling."

### CTA Copy
**Strong:**
- "Download free. No demo. No sales call."
- "Try it on your next job. Cancel anytime."
- "$X/month flat. See pricing."

**Weak:**
- "Book a demo to learn more."
- "Contact sales for custom pricing."
- "Request a free consultation."
