## Voice Calibration Notes

Study these patterns in the copy above:

**Sentence length:** Short by default. Long only when the detail earns it (testimonials, stories).

**Punctuation:** Periods dominate. Rare question marks. Almost zero exclamation points. Dashes for emphasis and rhythm.

**Capitalization:** ALL CAPS for section headlines only. Mixed case everywhere else. Never Title Case for body copy.

**Specificity:** "$1.6M in 4 years" not "rapid growth." "60 seconds" not "quickly." "2 hours daily" not "significant time savings."

**Honesty signals:** "Honestly?" "Fair question." "Here's the honest answer." These phrases build trust because they signal incoming candor.

**The quiet confidence:** Notice how the copy never claims to be the best. It shows. It proves. It lets the reader decide. That restraint IS the confidence.
